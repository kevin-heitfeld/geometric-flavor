### A.2 von Neumann Entropy

**Definition:**
```
S(ρ) = -Tr(ρ log ρ) = -Σᵢ λᵢ log λᵢ
```
where λᵢ are eigenvalues of ρ

**Properties:**
- S ≥ 0 (equality iff pure state)
- S ≤ log(d) (equality iff maximally mixed)
- S is invariant under unitary transformations
- S is concave function of ρ
