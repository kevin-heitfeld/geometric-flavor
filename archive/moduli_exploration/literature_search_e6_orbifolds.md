## 1. Classic Dixon et al. Papers (1985-1987)

- "Strings on Orbifolds" (Nucl. Phys. B 261, 1985)

- Systematic construction of 4D heterotic strings via orbifold compactification

- Focus on: Z_3, Z_4, Z_6, Z_7, Z_8, Z_12 single twist cases

**Relevant to us**:
- **Z_3 orbifold**: Gives E6 with 3 generations from œá = -6 ‚úì

- **Combined Z_3 √ó Z_4**: Not explicitly studied in these papers!

### Ib√°√±ez et al. (1987)

**Paper**: "Supersymmetry, Supergravity and Particle Physics"

## 3. Moduli Stabilization Literature

### KKLT (Kachru, Kallosh, Linde, Trivedi, 2003)

**Paper**: "De Sitter Vacua in String Theory"

**Results**:
- K√§hler moduli stabilized via non-perturbative effects
- Typical values: Im(T) ~ 1-3 in LARGE VOLUME limit
- Our Im(T) ~ 0.8 is SMALL but consistent with exponentially suppressed vacuum

### Denef & Douglas (2004)

**Paper**: "Distributions of Flux Vacua"

**Statistical approach**:
- Scan over ~10^500 flux vacua
- Distribution of moduli VEVs
- **Result**: Im(T) broadly distributed, peak at Im(T) ~ 1-2
- Our 0.8 is viable, just at lower end (~20th percentile)
