## What Works Across Successful Theories

### **✓ Renormalization Group (RG) Flow**
**Used by:** QCD, electroweak theory, statistical mechanics, condensed matter

**Why it works:**
- Systematically connects different scales
- Universal behavior near fixed points
- Small parameters naturally generated from running
- Predictive: calculate high energy from low energy (or vice versa)

**Success stories:**
- QCD coupling constant evolution
- Asymptotic freedom
- Critical phenomena (Ising model, etc.)
- ΛQCD emerges from dimensionless QCD

**Key insight:** Hierarchies can emerge from **logarithmic running** without fine-tuning

---

### **✓ Gauge Symmetry**
**Used by:** Standard Model, QCD, QED, general relativity (diffeomorphism invariance)

**Why it works:**
- Forces consistency (Ward identities, unitarity)
- Restricts form of interactions
- Explains charge conservation, massless photons
- Unification works (couplings converge)

**Success stories:**
- QED to 10 decimal places
- Electroweak unification
- QCD confinement and asymptotic freedom
- GR = gauge theory of diffeomorphisms

**Key insight:** **Symmetry principles constrain dynamics** powerfully

---

### **✓ Effective Field Theory (EFT)**
**Used by:** All modern physics

**Why it works:**
- Systematically includes all allowed terms
- Decoupling: high energy doesn't affect low energy (much)
- Predictive at each scale
- Explains why we can do physics without knowing everything

**Success stories:**
- Fermi theory → electroweak theory
- Chiral perturbation theory (QCD at low energy)
- Nuclear physics without knowing QCD details
- General relativity as EFT

**Key insight:** **Physics is scale-dependent**, deeper structure manifests as specific patterns in EFT parameters

---

### **✓ Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking (SSB)**
**Used by:** Higgs mechanism, QCD, superconductivity, magnets

**Why it works:**
- Generates structure from homogeneity
- Explains mass generation
- Goldstone bosons have predictive power
- Phase transitions natural

**Success stories:**
- Higgs mechanism (W, Z get mass)
- Chiral symmetry breaking in QCD
- Superconductivity (BCS theory)

**Key insight:** **Symmetry of laws ≠ symmetry of solutions**

---

### **✓ Universality / Attractors**
**Used by:** Statistical mechanics, RG flow, black holes

**Why it works:**
- Details don't matter, only universality class
- Many microscopic theories → same macroscopic behavior
- Robust predictions despite ignorance of details
- Explains why physics is possible

**Success stories:**
- Critical exponents (Ising, XY, etc. all give same exponents)
- Black hole entropy (many microscopics → same S = A/4)
- Fluid dynamics emerges from many microscopics
- RG fixed points

**Key insight:** **Universal behavior emerges from large classes of theories**

---

### **✓ Holography / Dimensional Reduction**
**Used by:** Black holes, AdS/CFT, cosmology

**Why it works:**
- Reduces degrees of freedom
- Entropy bounds
- Bulk physics encoded in boundary
- Explains why gravity is special

**Success stories:**
- Bekenstein-Hawking entropy
- AdS/CFT correspondence (proven in special cases)
- Holographic QCD approximations

**Key insight:** **Information content bounded by area, not volume**

## What Doesn't Work (Common Failure Modes)

### **❌ Too Many Free Parameters**
**Examples:** MSSM (105 parameters), most string compactifications

**Why it fails:**
- Not predictive
- Just curve fitting
- Can't be falsified (too flexible)
- Defeats purpose of theory

**Lesson:** **More parameters = less explanation**

---

### **❌ Putting In What You Want to Explain**
**Examples:** Froggatt-Nielsen charges, specific compactifications, anthropics

**Why it fails:**
- Circular reasoning
- Doesn't reduce mystery
- Just reformulates problem
- No deeper understanding

**Lesson:** **Output must not secretly be input**

---

### **❌ Fine-Tuning Masquerading as Explanation**
**Examples:** SUSY at high scale, split SUSY, string landscape

**Why it fails:**
- Requires extreme coincidences
- Naturalness crisis
- "Solves" problem by hiding it
- Not actually natural

**Lesson:** **If you need 1% tuning, you haven't solved the problem**

---

### **❌ Unfalsifiable Predictions**
**Examples:** Multiverse, string landscape, many anthropic arguments

**Why it fails:**
- Not science
- Explains everything = explains nothing
- No way to test
- Philosophical not physical

**Lesson:** **Theory must stick its neck out**

---

### **❌ No Connection to Observables**
**Examples:** Pure LQG, pure category theory approaches, some quantum gravity

**Why it fails:**
- Can't calculate anything measurable
- No contact with Standard Model
- Beautiful math, no physics
- Missing the point

**Lesson:** **Must connect to real experiments eventually**

---

### **❌ Assumes Continuum Spacetime**
**Examples:** Standard QFT, most BSM physics

**Why it fails (maybe):**
- Continuum → infinities
- Renormalization is band-aid
- Planck scale: spacetime probably discrete
- UV divergences suggest wrong degrees of freedom

**Lesson:** **Continuum might be emergent, not fundamental**

- **Resolution needed:** Continuum emergent from discrete substrate

### **Tension 5: Simplicity vs Complexity**
- Fundamental laws: Should be simple
- Observed physics: 26+ parameters, complex structure
- **Resolution needed:** Complex outputs from simple dynamics via nonlinearity/RG

```

- H_local = local qubit dynamics
- η = ONLY free parameter

#### **3. Coarse-Graining Map**
Merge nodes i,j if S(ρij) > η:
```
|Ψ_coarse⟩ = RG[|Ψ⟩]

- Reduces N → N/2 repeatedly

#### **5. Dynamics**
```
iℏ ∂|Ψ⟩/∂t = H|Ψ⟩
```
- Unitary evolution (standard QM)
- Generates entanglement naturally
- Triggers coarse-graining when S > η
